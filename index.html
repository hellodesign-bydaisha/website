About

Daisha is a designer driven by curiosity, craft, and a long-running love affair with making things tangible. Her work moves between fashion and the web, where silhouettes meet systems and materials meet interfaces. She's drawn to the in-between moments‚Äîthe draft, the prototype, the sample, the page just before it's polished. Currently studying fashion design as an adult, she's returning to a childhood dream with clearer eyes and steadier hands, bringing everything she's learned along the way with her. Detail, process, and intention are central to how she works. Her output is thoughtful, visual, and quietly playful, with room for softness, color, and personality. At the core, she believes creativity should feel alive and wearable‚Äîwhether it takes the form of a garment, a layout, or an idea finding its final shape.
